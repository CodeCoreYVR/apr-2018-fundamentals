Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Mix the ingredients in bowl.
  2. Roll out small balls of cookie dough on to a baking sheet.
  3. Place in the oven at 350°F for 20 minutes.
  4. Let cool for 10 minutes and enjoy!
